Reassured Mobile App usage policy

Introduction

The Reassured ltd mobile app has been created, and is supplied free of charge to you, as a Reassured LTD employee.
The views of users on the app do not in any way reflect the views of Reassured LTD as a company in any way.

Acceptable Use Policy

  • Language
    If you use this app, you must not use any foul language, the use of racism or sexism is also strictly prohibited, and your account will be disabled if you are found to be doing so.


  • Who may use the app
    You are permitted to use the app if you are a Reassured LTD employee. Once you leave the company, you are no longer permitted to use the app, and your account will be deactivated.


  • Content
    You must not share any company or client sensitive information through this app. If you are found to be doing so, your account will be disabled.


Privacy Policy

By using this app, you agree to the following privacy terms.
  • Name
    Your full name will be shared openly with all other users.
  • Office Location
    Your office location will be shared openly with all other users.
  • Your location
    By enabling the car sharing feature within this app, you agree to share your location with the server. Your location will be stored on the server each time another user requests to find a car share.
    If you are within 5 miles of another employee who is requesting a lift share, your name will be displayed in a list of employees.
    Your exact location will not be shared with other users, but will remain stored on our server.
    By setting "Offer Lift Share" to off in the lift sharing section, you will not be shown to other employees when they are using this feature. Your location will still be shared with our server.

    To stop sharing your location with our server, you must uninstall the app or disable location permissions for the app on your device.
